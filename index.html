About

Engineer & builder

I am an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (B.S.) student at UC Berkeley, focused on circuits, embedded systems, and signal processing. My work spans circuit design, PCB layout, and hardware-centered robotic systems, with an emphasis on building reliable devices that connect theory to real use.

Not just in engineering, I am also interested in physics, from mechanics to electromagnetism to quantum ideas. That curiosity still shapes how I approach problems. I like to understand how a system works first, then build from the fundamentals.

Outside of school, I enjoy creative work, photography, hiking, and exploring food culture.
